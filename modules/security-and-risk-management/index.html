Security and Risk Management Completed

Module Overview

Produced a defensible risk register, scenario analysis and BC/DR pack. Prioritisation balanced exploitability and business impact with outputs suited to both technical and executive audiences.

Key Submissions

  • Risk register & treatment plan — Likelihood/impact scoring with rationale; mapped to owners, objectives and verification steps.
  • Threat scenarios (ADTree) — Credential stuffing → lateral movement → exfiltration; layered mitigations across prevent/detect/respond.
  • BC/DR highlights — RTO/RPO per service; warm standby for Tier-1 workloads; recovery sequencing and clear comms flow.
