Module Overview

Executed risk management lifecycle (context, identification, analysis, treatment, monitoring). Tied decisions to appetite and constraints; delivered artefacts consumable by senior stakeholders.

Key Submissions

  • Risk register — Recorded assets, threats, vulnerabilities, impacts and likelihood; selected controls with owners and residual‑risk justification.
  • Board summary — Summarised top risks, treatments, cost/benefit and monitoring. Presented in non‑technical language aligned to objectives.

Supporting Artefacts

  • Threat‑modelling notes (STRIDE)
  • Statement of Applicability (draft)
  • KPI/KRI outline for control performance

Reflection

Improved at translating technical risk into business language and selecting controls that reduce risk measurably, not just theoretically.

Milestones

  • Define context and appetite
  • Identify threats and vulnerabilities
  • Score and prioritise risks
  • Select and plan treatments
  • Set up monitoring and review cadence

Skills Developed

  • Risk assessment
  • Control selection
  • Executive communication
  • Metrics
