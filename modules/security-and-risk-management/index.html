Security and Risk Management Completed

Module Overview

Executed risk management lifecycle. Delivered artefacts usable by both technical and executive stakeholders.

Key Submissions

  • Risk register — Assets, threats, vulnerabilities, likelihood/impact, controls, owners, and residual‑risk rationale.
  • Board summary — Top risks, treatments, cost/benefit and KPIs in plain language aligned to objectives/appetite.

Supporting Artefacts

  • Threat‑modelling notes (STRIDE lens)
  • Statement of Applicability (draft)
  • KPI/KRI outline for assurance

Reflection

Improved translation of technical risk into business‑ready decisions; selected controls that measurably reduce risk.

Milestones

  • Define context and appetite
  • Identify and rate risks
  • Select proportionate treatments
  • Plan monitoring and review

Skills Developed

  • Risk assessment
  • Control selection
  • Executive communication
  • Metrics
