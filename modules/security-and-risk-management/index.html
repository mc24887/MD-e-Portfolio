Security and Risk Management Completed

Module Overview

Applied risk identification, threat modelling and disaster recovery planning. Integrated seminar outputs and an executive summary with treatment decisions prioritised by business objectives.

Key Submissions

  • Risk Identification Report (PamperedPets) — assets, risk criteria, likelihood/impact scoring, prioritised treatment plan.
  • Threat Modelling (bank case) — STRIDE scenarios; mitigations and monitoring; concise board narrative.
  • Executive Summary & DR plan — RTO/RPO, comms plan, run‑books, test schedule for exercises.

Supporting Artefacts

  • Seminar notes & slides
  • Reflective portfolio linking risks to controls and outcomes
  • Peer‑evaluation highlights

Reflection

Turning findings into decisions (now/later/never) created the most value. Clear visuals + short narratives improved stakeholder alignment and rehearsal quality.
