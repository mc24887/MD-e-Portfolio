Reflection

This project reinforced the importance of integrating threat modelling, secure design, and layered testing from the outset. The OSS proposal served as a realistic business scenario where technical, organisational, and legal requirements had to be met. The combination of UML and STRIDE/DREAD improved the clarity of design decisions, while hands-on coding and security library integration built practical skills.
