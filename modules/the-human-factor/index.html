The Human Factor Completed

Module Overview

Analysed human‑centred risks and designed lightweight behavioural controls for a start‑up context.

Key Submissions

  • Critical analysis — password reuse, shadow IT, phishing susceptibility; prioritised realistic mitigations.
  • Presentation — micro‑training, just‑in‑time prompts, “report phish” UX, positive reinforcement.

Supporting Artefacts

  • Slides; peer‑review notes; 1‑page security champion run‑book

Reflection

Behaviour shifts with frictionless prompts + fast feedback far more than with long policy documents.

Milestones

  • Risk themes from incidents & surveys
  • Design nudges and feedback loops
  • Measure/report participation & outcomes

Skills Developed

  • Behavioural risk framing
  • Nudge design & awareness metrics
  • Supportive reporting culture

Evidence (selected)

Programme outline
  • Quarterly micro‑modules; monthly phishing exercises
  • “Report phish” button; 24‑hour response targets
  • Recognition for reporters; no‑blame write‑ups

Findings & Actions

FindingActionOutcome
Low report rateOne‑click report + fast feedbackReports ↑; dwell‑time ↓
Password reuseMFA + manager; targeted promptsReuse ↓; lockouts ↓
