The Human Factor Completed

Module Overview

Analysed human-centred risks (phishing susceptibility, password reuse, shadow IT) and designed low-friction, measurable interventions that improved behaviour without blame.

Key Submissions

  • Critical analysis — Compared three factors impacting start-ups; prioritised by exploitability/impact; designed targeted controls.
  • Presentation — Human-centric programme with role-based nudges, simple reporting and manager-led reinforcement.
Interventions & KPIs 
- Phish drills with instant micro-lessons
- Password manager rollout; SSO; conditional access
- JML checklist with automation; shadow IT amnesty
KPIs: report rate, completion %, phishing resilience, time-to-report

Supporting Artefacts

  • Essay & presentation deck with scenarios and metrics
  • Peer-review feedback and revisions

Reflection

Controls that reduce cognitive load achieved sustained improvements. Measuring outcomes (not attendance) kept the programme honest.

Milestones

  • Baseline metrics
  • Pilot interventions
  • Iterate & scale
  • Embed in onboarding/performance

Skills Developed

  • Metrics and A/B testing
