The Human Factor Completed

Module Overview

Studied behaviour, bias and culture. Designed interventions to nudge secure behaviour and measured outcomes beyond ‘click rate’.

Key Submissions

  • Awareness micro‑campaign — Delivered concise, role‑specific messages with clear calls‑to‑action; measured uplifts in desired behaviours.
  • Phishing drill analysis — Analysed false positives/negatives; segmented follow‑up coaching by need rather than blame.

Supporting Artefacts

  • Role personas and message tests
  • Comms schedule and reinforcement plan
  • KPI design for behaviour change, not vanity metrics

Reflection

Effective security communication is simple, relevant and timely. Empathy increased engagement and meaningful behaviour change.

Milestones

  • Baseline current behaviours
  • Co‑design messages with stakeholders
  • Pilot and adjust content
  • Run drill and collect evidence
  • Coach and reinforce

Skills Developed

  • Behavioural design
  • KPI setting
  • Empathy‑driven communication
