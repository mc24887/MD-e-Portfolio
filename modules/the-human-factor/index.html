The Human Factor Completed

Module Overview

Behavioural drivers of security outcomes; designed concise, role‑specific interventions and measured behaviour change beyond click‑rate.

Key Submissions

  • Awareness micro‑campaign — Targeted calls‑to‑action; measured uplift in desired behaviours and reduction in risky actions.
  • Phishing drill analysis — Assessed false positives/negatives; mapped coaching to needs; iterated materials.

Supporting Artefacts

  • Role personas and message tests
  • Comms schedule and reinforcement plan
  • Behaviour‑oriented KPIs and review cadence

Reflection

Empathy and clarity outperformed volume; coaching closed gaps faster than policy alone.

Milestones

  • Baseline behaviour and pain points
  • Co‑design and pilot messages
  • Run drill and collect evidence
  • Coach, reinforce and iterate

Skills Developed

  • Behavioural design
  • KPI setting
  • Empathy‑driven communication
